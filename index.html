Hey!Nobody had slightest of idea what it would be like, when it all started...

My first impression ....

I was fully bowled over for the charm and smile on her face..Oh god!! It's just not beautiful; it's awesome..

Yes I fell in love @ first sight of hers and decided to marry her without a second thought.. I was glad that she accepted and my joy had no bounds that day..

Yes it all happened over a CUP OF TEAAAA (and brunch :P).. From cars to bikes, passions to madness we shared our thoughts.. Her talks and cute giggles make me fall in love all over again...

Cupid struck both of us and decided to share the rest of our life's journey together..

I always wanna make her smile and will never ruin her mascara.. Since her smile makes me smile.. :):)

All was fine until I ....

Being a girl who only concentrated on achieving goals of my life... Focusing on being independent... Having a strong belief that there is no such magic called love...

Here I am holding a person's hand ready to fly for the entire life...Is it a dream or is it a magic which took place in seconds just over a CUP OF TEA.... Was it the spark in his eyes or the promising tone he had telling he would be holding my hand throughout?? I still have a question as to how I agreed? but the truth is angels are there showering their blessings and I am glad I have got my Mr.PERFECT....

A person who completes my incompleteness a person who stands by me in a crowd and still feels that I am his only princess and also withstands my childishness and madness.... I wish this dream of mine has no end.... The tender love he showers is much more enough to lead all ups and downs.....

Yes it is Sushil Damdhere who stole my heart and made me feel the essence of love.. :):)

 

It became clear...Our hearts have bought us to this place of total love by God's grace.. His gentle hand has touched us two and given us a love soooo true..

 

From that moment on...Together we dream together we live hand in hand we walk...
